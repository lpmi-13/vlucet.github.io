Research Interests

I am fascinated by Social-Ecological systems, and by how to model them. My master's thesis uses the landscape of Montéregie near Montréal to study how land use and land cover change impact the outcome of connectivity conservation efforts. I use classic methods of land use change modelling coupled with participatory methods of system modelling using Bayesian Belief Networks to answer my research questions.
